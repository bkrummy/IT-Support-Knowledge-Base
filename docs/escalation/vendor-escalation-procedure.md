# Vendor Escalation Procedure

## Objective
Provide a clear process for engaging third-party support when internal troubleshooting has been completed.

## Before Contacting Vendor
- Confirm issue is within vendor-supported scope
- Gather error messages and screenshots
- Gather timestamps and affected users
- Document troubleshooting already performed
- Collect version, device, and environment details

## Vendor Ticket Content
- Clear problem summary
- Business impact
- Reproduction steps
- Logs or screenshots if allowed
- Internal ticket reference
- Requested urgency

## After Escalation
- Record vendor case number
- Update internal stakeholders
- Track next action and owner

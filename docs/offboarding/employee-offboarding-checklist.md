# Employee Offboarding Checklist

## Objective
Remove access in a timely, controlled manner and recover company assets while preserving business continuity.

## Inputs Required
- Employee full name
- Termination date and time
- Manager name
- HR approval or workflow trigger
- Device assignment details
- Mailbox and file ownership transition requirements

## Checklist
- Confirm approved termination request
- Identify all assigned accounts and systems
- Disable primary account at approved time
- Revoke VPN and remote access
- Remove MFA methods if applicable
- Reset or transfer shared credentials according to policy
- Transfer mailbox or shared file ownership as approved
- Notify asset recovery owner
- Recover laptop, badge, accessories, and other assigned equipment
- Update asset inventory records
- Document completion in ticket or HR workflow

## Completion Criteria
Access is removed, assets are recovered or accounted for, and ownership handoffs are documented.

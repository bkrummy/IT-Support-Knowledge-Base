# Privileged Access Guidelines

## Objective
Reduce risk by controlling elevated access requests.

## Principles
- Least privilege
- Time-bound access where possible
- Approval by authorized manager or system owner
- Separate standard and administrative accounts when policy requires
- Review and removal of stale privileged access

## Requirements
- Business justification
- Proper approval
- Scope of requested access
- Duration if temporary
- Ticket reference for auditability

## Escalate or Reject When
- Request lacks approval
- Request is broader than job responsibilities
- Emergency access is requested without incident context

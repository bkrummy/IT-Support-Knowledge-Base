# Incident Severity Guidelines

## Severity 1 - Critical
Broad business impact, major outage, or security-critical event.

## Severity 2 - High
Significant impact to a team or important function with no good workaround.

## Severity 3 - Medium
Single-user or limited issue with moderate business impact.

## Severity 4 - Low
Minor issue, informational request, or issue with easy workaround.

## Notes
Severity should reflect business impact, not only technical complexity.
